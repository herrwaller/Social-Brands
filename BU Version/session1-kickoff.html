The 1st Law of Branding

品牌第一法则

As markets become saturated and products increasingly similar, consumers cannot rely on functional differentiation alone. This is the central business problem that brands were invented to solve. The Pepsi Challenge (1975/1983) illustrated this strikingly: in blind taste tests, consumers preferred Pepsi over Coca-Cola. But in open tests — with brands visible — Coke won decisively. The brand's emotional associations overrode taste perception entirely.

随着市场日趋饱和、产品越来越相似，消费者无法仅靠功能差异做出判断。这是品牌诞生的核心商业问题。百事挑战（1975/1983年）鲜明地说明了这一点：在盲测中，消费者更偏好百事可乐；但在展示品牌标识的开放测试中，可口可乐以绝对优势胜出。品牌的情感联想完全凌驾于味觉感知之上。

"The less perceptible the functional differences between products, the more important the emotional components of brands become. That is our entry ticket into the business world — and our reason for existence."

产品间功能差异越难被感知，品牌的情感成分就越重要。这是我们进入商业世界的入场券——也是我们存在的理由。

In saturated markets with largely interchangeable products and services, advertising becomes indispensable — it contributes to the positioning and profiling of products and services against competition (Schenk 2005: 91). This is not just a marketing insight; it is the foundational argument for why brands matter — and why the ethical dimensions of branding matter equally.

在产品与服务高度同质化的饱和市场中，广告变得不可或缺——它通过定位与形象塑造，帮助产品和服务在竞争中脱颖而出（Schenk 2005: 91）。这不仅是一个营销洞见，更是品牌重要性的基础论据——也是品牌伦理同等重要的原因。

第四节

What Brands Are

品牌是什么

Three progressive statements — each one expanding the definition:

三个递进的论断——每一个都扩展了品牌的定义：

Statement 1
  • A brand is more than a logo. Apple's logo is recognizable worldwide — but Apple's brand is the ecosystem, the culture, the philosophy of "think different."
  • 品牌不仅仅是一个标志。苹果的标志举世闻名——但苹果品牌是整个生态系统、文化与"非同凡想"的哲学。
Statement 2
  • A brand is more than an advertising campaign. The iPod silhouette ads were iconic — but Apple's brand persists across and beyond any single campaign.
  • 品牌不仅仅是一个广告活动。iPod剪影广告具有标志性——但苹果的品牌跨越并超越了任何一次单独的广告活动。
Statement 3
  • A brand is even more than a product. Products change across iPhone generations — the brand remains consistent. The brand is what customers are actually buying.
  • 品牌甚至超越了产品本身。不同代iPhone的产品在变化——品牌却始终如一。品牌才是顾客真正购买的东西。

A brand is a holistic identity concept that is implemented across all departments, builds trust, and makes brand-driven companies successful in all areas of their business.

品牌是一个整体性身份概念，在所有部门中得到贯彻实施，建立信任，并使品牌驱动型企业在其业务的所有领域都取得成功。

Brand & Sustainability

品牌与可持续性

Brands today must account for their full value chain. In the holistic sense of brand development and company policy, sustainability also includes sustainable production conditions and supply chains. This is not merely an ethical imperative — it is increasingly a business one.

当今品牌必须对其整个价值链负责。从整体品牌发展与企业政策的意义上说，可持续性也包括可持续的生产条件与供应链。这不仅仅是道德命令——它越来越成为一种商业必要。

第六节

Business & Society

商业与社会

"Economic activity always has consequences for society."

经济活动始终对社会产生影响。

Companies and brands must demonstrate their legitimacy at a superordinate level — obtaining societal acceptance in the sense of a "license to operate." This creates the central tension of the course: the traditional purpose of branding is to get more people to buy more things, for more years, at a higher price. But is this compatible with ethical and social responsibility?

企业与品牌必须在更高层面证明其存在的合理性——获得社会认可，即"运营许可证"。这构成了本课程的核心张力：品牌传统目的是让更多人购买更多产品、持续更多年、以更高价格。但这与伦理责任和社会责任相容吗？

A brand must simultaneously justify its existence to society (legitimation), be relevant to individuals (relevance), and stand out from competitors (differentiation). In this model, CSR and brand purpose are not optional add-ons — they are the foundation of societal legitimation.

品牌必须同时向社会证明其存在的合理性（合法性）、与个体保持相关性（相关性），并从竞争对手中脱颖而出（差异化）。在此模型中，CSR与品牌使命并非可选的附加项——它们是社会合法性的基础。

The goal of this seminar: to develop a fundamental brand understanding by placing brands in a broader social context — and to identify our own social responsibility in brand development. The key questions: What do brands of the future look like? What responsibility must and can brands and companies take on?

本研讨课的目标：通过将品牌置于更广泛的社会语境中，培养基本的品牌理解——并明确我们在品牌发展中的社会责任。核心问题：未来的品牌是什么样的？品牌与企业必须并能够承担什么样的责任？

第七节

Business Ethics: An Introduction

商业伦理：导论

For more than twenty years, there has been a "business ethics boom" (Wirtschaftsethikboom) in academic discourse — a seemingly permanent high season for the field. This is not surprising: every major economic scandal, environmental disaster, and working condition exposé renews the call for ethical accountability in business.

二十余年来，学术讨论中出现了"商业伦理热潮"（Wirtschaftsethikboom）——该领域仿佛处于持续的高峰期。这并不令人惊讶：每一次重大经济丑闻、环境灾难与劳工条件曝光，都会重新引发对商业道德问责的呼声。

"As a manager, my goal is to serve a higher good. I will act with the utmost integrity and accomplish my work in a morally impeccable way."

作为管理者，我的目标是服务于更高的善。我将以最高的诚信行事，以道德无可指摘的方式完成我的工作。

MBA Oath, Harvard Business School (cited after Noll 2013: 83)

But the gap between aspiration and reality is wide. Consider: Despite well-known consequences of CO₂ emissions and climate change, lobbyists from the energy and automotive industries continue to prevent the implementation of truly deep reforms. SUVs remain on the rise. According to the World Food Programme (UN), there is enough food in the world to feed the entire global population — yet 925 million people suffer from hunger, because food is so unequally distributed.

然而，理想与现实之间的差距依然巨大。试看：尽管CO₂排放与气候变化的后果已广为人知，能源与汽车行业的游说者仍在成功阻止真正深刻改革的推进。SUV依然大行其道。根据联合国世界粮食计划署，世界上有足够的粮食养活全体人类——然而有9.25亿人饥寒交迫，因为粮食分配极为不均。

The Contradiction
矛盾所在
  • The main purpose of branding is traditionally: get more people to buy more stuff, for more years, at a higher price
  • 品牌传统上的核心目的是：让更多人购买更多产品、持续更多年、以更高价格
  • Economic actors are incentivized to externalize costs — to the environment, workers, society
  • 经济主体有动力将成本外部化——转嫁给环境、工人和社会
  • Climate damage, inequality, and social harm are often the price of profit
  • 气候破坏、不平等与社会伤害往往是利润的代价
The Opportunity
机遇所在
  • Brands that embed ethical responsibility into their core identity build more durable trust
  • 将道德责任嵌入核心身份的品牌，能够建立更持久的信任
  • Purpose-driven brands outperform purely profit-driven ones in long-term value
  • 使命驱动型品牌在长期价值上优于纯粹利润驱动型品牌
  • CSR is not just ethics — it is competitive advantage
  • CSR不只是道德——它是竞争优势

"Branding without a stance is fraud." — Brand conference

"没有立场的品牌是欺骗。"——品牌大会
